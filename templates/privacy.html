Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

We collect information such as your name, email address, resume details, educational background, skills, work experience, and interview responses to provide personalized AI-based career services.

Your information is used to analyze resumes, generate ATS scores, identify skill gaps, provide interview preparation, and offer personalized career recommendations to improve your job readiness.

We use cookies and trusted third-party services to improve website performance, enhance user experience, provide secure authentication, and analyze platform usage. These services follow their own privacy and security policies.

Data Protection :

We apply appropriate security measures to protect your personal information and uploaded resumes from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Your data is kept confidential and is not sold to third parties.

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials. We recommend using strong passwords and protecting your login information to keep your account secure.

User Rights ;

Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal information, remove uploaded resumes, and request account deletion whenever required.
